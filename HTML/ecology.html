ECOLOGY THE STUDY OF ORGANISMS & THE ENVIRONMENT

As we learn more and more of the impact humans are having on the planet, the more important it becomes that scientists explore how to create a sustainable future not just for humans but for the whole planet.

In our ecology section of the museum, we unpack the dangers we currently face including global warming, extinction of species, and pollution. But we also focus on the positive ways we can all contribute to a brighter future.

Did you know...

...that humans release over 40 billion tons of carbon dioxide into the atmosphere each year, primarily from burning fossil fuels? This contributes to the greenhouse effect, which is causing the Earth's temperature to rise and leading to climate change.

Did you know...

...that global warming has caused the Arctic to lose nearly 95% of its oldest and thickest ice in just the past 30 years?

Did you know...

...that a single tree can absorb as much as 48 pounds of carbon dioxide per year and can provide enough oxygen for two people?

A BETTER TOMORROW

Our exhibit offers a captivating journey through the intricate balance of our planet's ecosystems. Discover the fascinating science behind our environment and gain a deeper understanding of the delicate relationships between living organisms and their surroundings.

Explore a world of interactive displays, stunning visuals and immersive experiences that will inspire you to take action and become a steward of our planet. Discover the critical role that each of us plays in preserving our natural world for future generations. Join us in our mission to promote awareness and inspire positive change for a better tomorrow.
