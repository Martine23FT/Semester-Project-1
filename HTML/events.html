AERONAUTICS WITH PROF SHIELA WIDNALL

It is our pleasure to announce that professor Shiela Widnall from the Massachusetts Institute of Technology will be delivering 3 lectures on the development of aeronautics and where the future lies in this exciting “space”.

NIGHT AT THE MUSEUM

Experience the museum in a whole new light with Night at the Museum! Join us after hours for a fun-filled evening of exploration, entertainment, and a stunning lightshow that brings the exhibits to life. Don't miss out on this unique and unforgettable adventure!

ENERGETICA EXHIBITION

On loan from the NEMO Science Museum in Amsterdam, the Energetica exhibiton is coming to the Community Science Museum. It's a series of installations that allow visitors to experience the power of the elements as we harness them.
